How to Use This Command Center

Management Team Toolkits: These are your role-specific applications. Click PM TOOLKIT for project management tools including schedule analysis and earned value tracking. Click QCM TOOLKIT for quality control and submittal management. Click SSHO TOOLKIT for safety planning and compliance.

Operations & Resources: Quick access to essential platforms. The Advanced Development Toolkit button opens your workspace for custom tool development and workflow automation. The Google Drive button provides direct access to all project files, documents, and shared resources.

Client Portals: Each federal agency has unique requirements and specifications. Select USACE for Army Corps of Engineers projects with CQC requirements and design-build documentation. Choose NAVFAC for Naval Facilities Engineering Command standards and UFC guidelines. Select VA for Veterans Affairs healthcare facility projects with FGI Guidelines and infection control requirements. Click STATE AGENCIES for state-specific building codes and regional compliance documentation.

Reference Documents: Quick access to essential specifications, standards, and regulations. This section provides immediate access to UFGS specifications, UFC building requirements, FGI Guidelines, NFPA codes, OSHA standards, and agency-specific quality control and safety plans.

Mission Tracking: Use the LOCAL TIME clock to stay synchronized with project schedules across time zones. The SESSION TIMER automatically starts when you open the Command Center and tracks your current work session - click Pause to take breaks, Start to resume, and Reset when starting a new task or project phase. This helps you accurately track time spent on specific activities for project documentation and billing purposes.

Navigation: Click any card to access that toolkit or portal. Use the back button on each page to return to this main command center. All tools are designed to work together seamlessly, allowing you to move between project management, quality control, and safety compliance functions as your work requires.
